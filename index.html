About Me

About me

My name is Kevin Qi, I am a computer science graduate and a recent software development bootcamp graduate. I am also a full-stack developer. I know many frontend and backend frameworks and also proficient in Javascript, React, and Python. Besides that, I have more than one year of working experience that relates to web development and one month of experience as a front-end mobile engineering intern.

After graduating from college, I completed two internships that relate to web development. The first one I worked as a Web Content Manager Intern in an ecommerce startup company and I helped the company built two online store websites for their projects using a content management system called Squarespace. For the second internship, I worked in a startup as a web developer intern, and i was responsible for developing and managing their Wordpress website. Besides that, before I graduated from the software development bootcamp. I also helped a startup company to build an ios App. I implemented the features that users can fully reserve an event or buy tickets for the event by using React-Native, Redux, and Javascript.

With all these experiences under my belt, now I’m looking for an opportunity to apply my technical experience and my creative problem solving skills at an innovative software company.
